# Final Project Proposal

Write a proposal of sufficient length (1-3 pages) for your final project.

## Address the Following

### Motivation and Goals

- What do you want to accomplish and why?
- How will this help you develop the programming skills you will need in the future?

### Methodology

- What data will you use? How will you get the data?
- What is your plan to carry out this project?
- How will you accomplish the programming tasks? What libraries or tools will you use?

### Evaluation

- How will you ensure accuracy? (e.g., manual evaluation, comparing to a gold standard, etc.)
- How will you evaluate the success of your project?

### Intangibles

- Which parts of the project feel unclear?
- What do you need to learn to be able to complete the project?

## Project Expectations

Design a project that will challenge you without being so ambitious that you risk failure. It should be equivalent to at least 3 weeks' worth of assignments in terms of the amount of time required to complete the project.

Be sure that you have a good idea of how to achieve your goal from the outset, but don't be afraid to incorporate techniques that we have not learned yet; I will help you determine if they are too ambitious. We can dedicate some class time to learning tools or techniques specific to your project (topic modeling, sentiment analysis, LLM APIs (you will have to pay for a subscription), etc.).

If you have questions, please come chat with me in my office or send an email.
